Prima,

Ghorite jokhon 12 ta baje, arekta bochor chole jay, arekta suru hoy - kintu ei muhurte shudhu ekta kotha mone hoy... tumi acho bole ei prithibi ta aro sundor lagteche amar kache.

Aj tomar 21 tomo birthday. Mone porteche 2022 e prothom kotha shuru hoyechilo phone e, jante o partam na je ekdin tumi amar jibone erokom jaygay chole ashbe. Stk te prothom dekhar agey tumi bolechila na - "ei meet er por amader valobasha barbe naile kombe." Aj bollam, shetai agiyeche... onek dure.

Chandronath er sei paharer choto trail theke shuru kore, Eco park er ghono jongole, Chillox ba Pizzaburg er hashi thaka adda, tomar amar basay ashar sei din - protita muhurto mone ache. Ar hm, sei dairge wala picture er kotha mone ache to? Aj o tomake khepate iccha korche oi niye, kintu ajke tomar din - to aj shudhu valobashai thakbe.

Jani ekhon amra duijonei onek pressure e thaki. Tumi grame, ami ekhane... biyer kotha mone hole mone hoy kobe hobe, kemon hobe. Kintu jano ki Prima? Tumi amar jonno jotogula sacrifice korcho, male friends theke shuru kore sob kichu chere diyecho shudhu amader relationship er jonno - ei jinis gula amake aro beshi bhalobashte shekhai tomake. Ei juge loyalty, genuine valobasha khuje paowa mushkil... kintu tumi seta ajke o biswas koro, ar ami o tomar biswas ta kono din vangbo na - ei promise rakhbo shob cheyeo upore.

Aj theke tumi 21. Aro boro hoye jaccho, ar amader golpo tao aro boro hobe ekdin. Jor kore raji koresi tomake amar basha te ashar jonno, seta hole tomar hat e sei Cox's Bazar er shamuk er kholos gula dite parbo. Kintu present er cheyeboro kotha hocche - ami cahchi tomar pashe thakte. Shob lomba texting, chhoto message, casual kotha ba romantic muhurto - shob e tumi thako bole shob kichu meaningful.

Kathgolap er moto tumi amar jonno special. Ar jemon golap dile tumi khushi hou, temni ami cahchi jibon ta diye toke khushi rakhte.

Happy 21st Birthday, Prima. Ei bochor ta tomar jonno aro sundor hok, ar amader golpo ta aro agiye jak.
